Our Availiable Trips

We currently have 2 expansive and different rivers availiable for rafting. If you have any questions, or would like to reserve a trip, please contact us here to get started!

Trip About the Trip Length
Big and Proud Our big and Proud is, as it's name suggests, the biggest and our proudest river we have availiable. It is ~40 miles long, and with multiple drop-off points, can be split into a half-day trip, a whole-day trip, and for those brave enough, can be completed with a two day trip, with camping spots availiable on the river. 2 days
Fast 'Nelly While our Big and Proud is our most scenic river, the Fast 'Nelly is our second river, and contains more rapids for those who seek a little more action on the river. It is ~35 miles, but with it's rapids can be completed in about a day and a half, and like the Big and Proud can be split into smaller trips to ocamidate everyone. One and a Half days
Half day trips Our half day trips last for about 8 miles and be determined on what kind of trip you wish. It can be tailored for scenic trips or more rapid if thats what you want. Half day trips are good for those who don't want to spend the whole day on the river. ~3 hours
Scenic trips Our scenic trips mostly take place on our Big and Proud, and have many wonderful views like a view of our nearby mountain, a vast valley, and has some spots in a chasm, with some canyon views. The water is typically calmer but will contain some rapids to really include everyone. tbd
Whole day trips Our whole day trips typically take about 6-8 hours to complete and can be tailored for whatever experience you and your group wish to have on the water. It usually travels about 15 miles and have many differnet spots to step off and enjoy the scene around you. 6-8 hours.
